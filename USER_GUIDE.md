plan **activities**, invite people over **WhatsApp**, and hit your **goals**.

## 1. Getting started

When you open BizTrack you land on the **login screen**. You have three ways in:

### Try the demo (no account needed)
Click **“Explore the demo”**. This loads the whole app with realistic sample data
(customers, partners, activities, goals) that lives only in your browser. Nothing is
saved to a server — it’s the best way to look around. You’ll see a **DEMO MODE** badge
in the sidebar. Sign out any time to leave.

### Log in
Enter your **Username** and **Password** and press **Sign in**.

### Register a new account
Switch to **Create your account** and fill in:
- **Display name** — how your name appears in the app
- **Username** — what you log in with
- **Email** *(optional)*
- **Upline username** *(optional)* — the person who sponsors you. This links you into
  the network so your upline can see your activity. Leave blank if you’re at the top.
- **Password** (min 6 characters) and confirm it

> **Tip:** Your **upline** determines who sees your records. You and everyone below you
> in the network can be seen by those above you; activities are shared with everyone.

## 2. Getting around

The **left sidebar** is your main menu:

| Menu | What it’s for |
|------|---------------|
| **Dashboard** | Your daily overview |
| **Activities** | Calendar of events, meetings, launches |
| **Customers** | People who buy from you |
| **Partners** | Your downline / network |
| **Purchases** | Orders and sales |
| **My Goals** | Targets you’re working toward |
| **Products** | Product catalog & pricing reference |

On a **phone**, tap the **☰ menu** icon in the top-left to open the sidebar.
**Sign out** is at the bottom of the sidebar.

---

## 3. Dashboard

Your home screen, with a greeting and a snapshot of everything:

- **Stat cards** — Upcoming Activities, Active Partners, Prospects, Active Goals
- **Upcoming Activities** — your next events; click any one to jump straight to it on
  the calendar
- **Goals** — progress bars for your active goals
- **Your Network** — counts of Active / Prospects / Unassigned partners, plus a shortcut
  to the **relationship tree**
- **Recent Orders** — your latest sales with amount and status

Every panel has a link (e.g. **Calendar →**, **View all →**) to the full section.

## 4. Customers

**Customers** are people who buy products from you (not necessarily in your network).

### View & search
The Customers page lists everyone with their contact details. Use the **search box** to
filter by name, email, or phone.

### Add a customer
Click **Add Customer** and fill in name, email, phone, company, address, and notes.
Only the name is required.

### Customer detail
Click a customer to open their profile, where you can:
- **Edit** (pencil icon) or **Delete** (trash icon) the customer
- See their **purchase history**
- **Add a purchase** for them directly

---

## 5. Purchases

The **Purchases** page is the full list of orders across all customers.

Each order records:
- **Customer**
- **Product name**
- **Amount** (shown in RM)
- **Purchase date**
- **Status** — *pending*, *completed*, or *cancelled*

You can add a purchase here, or from a specific customer’s detail page (which pre-fills
the customer for you).

## 6. Partners & your network

**Partners** are the people in your downline — the network you’re building.

### The partner list
- Two tabs: **All Partners** and **Unassigned** (partners with no sponsor set)
- **Search** by name, email, phone, or rank
- **Add Partner** to create a new one
- **View Tree** to see the network diagram

### Add / edit a partner
Open the form (via **Add Partner**, or the pencil on a partner’s detail) and set:
- **Name** *(required)*
- **Email**, **Phone**
- **Status** — active or prospect
- **Account Type** — **ABO** (Business Owner) or **ABC** (Business Customer)
- **Network Position** and **Rank / Pin Level**
- **Sponsor (Upline)** — who this partner sits under
- **Partner’s Partner** — e.g. a spouse also in the business
- **Interest Tags** — comma-separated (e.g. `Health, Skincare, Recruiting`). These power
  the smart **suggested partners** feature when you plan activities.
- **Notes**

### Partner detail
Clicking a partner opens a side panel with their info. From here you can:
- **Edit** or **Delete**
- **Message on WhatsApp** (if they have a phone number)
- See their **sponsor**, **interest tags**, and the **activities** they’re linked to
- Use the **‹ ›** arrows or arrow keys to flip through partners

### Relationship tree
**View Tree** (or **View relationship tree** on the Dashboard) draws your whole network
as a diagram — who sponsors whom, top to bottom. Great for seeing your organization at
a glance.

## 7. Activities & the calendar

**Activities** are your meetings, product launches, trainings, recruiting days, workouts —
anything you schedule. This is the heart of BizTrack.

### The calendar
The month view shows each day with **event chips** — small color-coded pills showing the
time and title of each activity (all-day events show no time). Colors match the activity
**category**. If a day has more events than fit, you’ll see **“+N more”**.

- **Move between months** with the **‹ ›** arrows; **Today** jumps back to the current month.
- **Click a day** to see everything scheduled on it in the panel on the right.
- **Click an event chip** to open that activity’s full details.

### Create an activity
Click **New Activity** and fill in:
- **Title** *(required)*
- **Description**
- **Category** — Meeting, Training, Product Launch, Recruiting, Social, Recognition,
  Workout, Sport, Wellness, or **Other** (type your own)
- **Status** — planned, completed, or cancelled
- **All day** — tick this for a whole-day event (hides the time field)
- **Date** *(required)* and **Time** — click anywhere in the field to open the picker
- **Address** — a real address; BizTrack looks it up and drops a **map pin** automatically
- **Tags** — comma-separated interests (e.g. `Health, Business Building`). These are matched
  against your partners’ interest tags to suggest who to invite.

### Activity detail
Click an activity (chip or day-agenda item) to open its detail panel:
- Date/time, description, tags, and a **map** of the location
- **Open in Google Maps** for directions
- **Invited Partners** with their RSVP status — mark each **confirmed** ✓, **pending** ⏱,
  message them on WhatsApp, or remove them
- **Suggested Partners** — people whose interests match this activity (or who are open to
  everything); invite them in one tap
- Toolbar: **Edit** ✎, **Duplicate** ⧉ (make a copy to reschedule), **Delete** 🗑, **Close** ✕
- Flip between the day’s activities with the **‹ ›** arrows or arrow keys

### Bulk upload activities
Planning many events at once? Click **Bulk Upload**:
1. **Download the Excel template** — columns are **Title, Description, Date, Time, Address,
   Category, Tags**.
2. Fill in one row per activity.
3. Upload the file. BizTrack validates each row (flagging any missing titles/dates),
   looks up addresses for the map, and imports them.

## 8. Inviting partners over WhatsApp

Anywhere you see the **WhatsApp** icon (partner detail, invited partners, suggested
partners), BizTrack opens WhatsApp with a **ready-to-send invite** already written —
including the activity’s title, date, time, and location.

For the **Invite** button on suggested partners, BizTrack both opens the WhatsApp message
**and** records the invitation, so it shows up under Invited Partners as *pending*.

**Phone number formats** — both work:
- **Local Malaysian:** `012-345 6789` → automatically sent as `+60…`
- **Another country:** start with a **`+` and country code**, e.g. `+65 9375 8894` for
  Singapore. When you include the `+`, BizTrack uses that number exactly as-is.

## 9. Goals

**My Goals** lets you set targets and watch your progress.

Create a goal with:
- **Goal Title** *(required)* — e.g. *Reach RM 100k revenue*
- **Description**
- **Category**
- **Unit** — RM, qty, %, customers, etc.
- **Target Value** and **Current Value**
- **Deadline**
- **Status** — Active, Paused, or Completed

Each goal shows a **progress bar** (current ÷ target). Active goals also appear on your
Dashboard. Update the current value as you make progress; at 100% the bar turns green.

## 10. Product catalog

**Products** is a reference catalog you can browse and search by **category**:
**Energy, Beauty, Personal Care, Home Living, Home Care**.

Each product shows its image, description, and pricing for **Singapore** and **Malaysia**,
including the **retail** vs **member (ABO)** price and how much a member saves. Use it as a
quick reference when talking to customers or partners.

## 11. Tips & FAQ

**Is my demo data saved?**
No. Demo mode is entirely in your browser with sample data — nothing is stored on a server,
and it resets. Register or log in to keep real records.

**The date/time picker wouldn’t open on my laptop.**
Click **anywhere inside** the date or time field — the calendar/time picker opens on click,
not only on the little icon.

**A WhatsApp link said the number “isn’t on WhatsApp”.**
Check the phone number format. Malaysian numbers can be entered locally (`012-345 6789`);
for any other country, include the `+` and country code (e.g. `+65…`). See
[section 8](#8-inviting-partners-over-whatsapp).

**Who can see my records?**
Records are scoped to you and your downline — your upline can see your and your team’s
records. **Activities are shared with everyone** so the whole organization can coordinate.

**How do I sign out?**
Use **Sign out** at the bottom of the sidebar.
